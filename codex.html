The Y Combinator Codex

The Y Combinator Codex is a calligraphy piece I've created.

  • Creation time/location: January 13th-25th 2021, Kraków, Poland.
  • Size: 420mm x 2400mm (42cm x 2.4m), plus white margins on both short sides (about 10cm each).
  • Tools used: 420mm 120g/m² plotter paper, Pilot Parallel calligraphy pens, Hero and Pelikan inks, Ecoline watercolor paints, Ecoline metallic gold paint, Vallejo metallic silver paint.
  • Inspiration: the Y Combinator Codex by emacsomancer.
  • Condition: very good (wrinkles near the paper margins, one 3cm tear over a green flourish - see photos).

It will be possible to buy printed copies and bid for the original piece during European Lisp Symposium 2021 with all financial gains being donated to Lisp-related organizations. The web service for facilitating this will be announced during the conference.
